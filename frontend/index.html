How to Use Lunar

1
Upload your slidesDrop any PDF, PPTX, DOCX, or image — even photos of slides. Lunar reads every slide (OCR included).
2
Step into the classroomYour real slide opens on the board and Lunar teaches it one at a time — clear explanations and worked examples, only from your content.
3
Highlight → Explain or SolveSelect any part of the slide or the lesson, then tap Explain for a closer look, or Solve to work a question step by step. You can also just ask Lunar anything in the box.
4
Find anythingType a slide number or any keyword in the search box to jump straight to that slide.
5
At your pace, on your deviceHit Stop to cut a reply short, use Previous / Next to move. Everything runs locally — no keys, no internet, nothing leaves your machine.
