How it works

Three steps
to your private class.

1

Upload your slides

Drag in any lecture file — PDF, PowerPoint, Word, even photos of slides. Lunar reads every slide, OCR included.

2

Step into the classroom

Your real slide opens on the board and Lunar teaches it one at a time — clear explanations and worked examples, grounded only in your content.

3

Highlight, ask, or jump anywhere

Highlight any part of a slide or the lesson and tap Explain or Solve, ask Lunar anything in your own words, or use Find anything to jump to any slide by number or keyword. Hit Stop the moment you've heard enough.
