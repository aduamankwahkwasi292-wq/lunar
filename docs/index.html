Why it's different

Not a chatbot. A class
that's just for you.

Taught slide by slide

Your real slide opens on the board and Lunar teaches it like a patient professor — grounded only in your material, nothing invented.

Box-select → Explain or Solve

Drag a box around any part of a slide. Lunar breaks it down, or works the question step by step — exactly what you selected, nothing else.

Find anything, instantly

Jump to any slide by number or keyword across your whole deck — your notes, fully searchable, in a blink.

100% on your device

The AI runs entirely on your machine. No API keys, no sign-up, no internet. Your slides never leave your computer.

How it works

Three steps to your
private class.

01

Upload your slides

Drop a PDF, PowerPoint, Word doc, or even photos of slides. Lunar reads every page — OCR included.

02

Step into the classroom

Your slide opens on the board and Lunar teaches it, one at a time, with clear explanations and worked examples.

03

Box-select, ask, or jump

Box anything for Explain or Solve, ask Lunar in your own words, or search to jump anywhere in the deck.
