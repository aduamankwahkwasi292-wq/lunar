How it works

Three steps to
your private class.

1

Upload your slides

Drop a PDF, PowerPoint, Word doc, or even photos of slides. Lunar reads every page — OCR included.

2

Step into the classroom

Your slide opens on the hologram board and Lunar teaches it, one at a time, with clear explanations and worked examples.

3

Box-select, ask, or jump

Box anything for Explain or Solve, ask Lunar in your own words, or search to jump anywhere in the deck.
